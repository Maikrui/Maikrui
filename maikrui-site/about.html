About Maikruiworkshop

We are a professional workshop of custom metal hardware based in Quanzhou, China. With over 10 years of experience in belt buckles, garment rivets, and custom CNC parts, we provide OEM/ODM services for brands worldwide.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, cost-effective hardware solutions with fast lead times and reliable customer support.
