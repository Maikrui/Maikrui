About Maikrui Workshop

We are an integrated source factory with over 30 years of experience in garment hardware manufacturing.

Our core strength is zinc alloy die casting, supported by spin casting, lost wax casting and stamping processes to cover all your order needs.

With in-house mold design, CNC machining, plating, epoxy filling and laser engraving, we deliver consistent quality and reliable lead times.

We specialize in custom belt buckles, belt rivets and a full range of garment hardware accessories, serving global buyers worldwide.
