About Maikrui Workshop

We are an integrated factory with over 30 years of experience in garment hardware.

Our core strength is zinc alloy die casting, supported by spin casting, lost wax casting and stamping.

We control full production in-house, including mold design, CNC machining, plating, epoxy filling and laser engraving.

We specialize in custom belt buckles, rivets and garment hardware for global buyers.

Our Capabilities

  • Zinc Alloy Die Casting
  • Spin Casting & Lost Wax Casting
  • Stamping
  • CNC Machining
  • Plating, Painting & Polishing
  • Laser Engraving & Custom Finishes
